================================================================================
PROBLEM FIXED
================================================================================

BEFORE:
Long Gemini responses were truncated in Bedrock chat:
"[Gemini] Here's what Minecraft is: a sandbox video game where players..."
(rest of message was cut off)

AFTER:
Long responses are now split into multiple readable parts:
"[Gemini] Here's what Minecraft is: a sandbox video game where players"
"can build and explore environments made of blocks, either by themselves"
"or in groups. Players can gather resources, craft items, build structures,"
"and battle mobs."

================================================================================
WHAT CHANGED
================================================================================

1. main.js (Lines 172-183)
   - Now uses: formatMultilineResponse() function
   - Splits responses into readable parts
   - Sends each part as separate message
   - Logs how many parts were created

2. messageFormatter.js (Lines 107-169)
   - Completely rewrote formatMultilineResponse()
   - Smart word-boundary breaking
   - 320 character limit per message
   - Separate formatting for first message and continuations
   - Safety limit of 50 parts maximum

================================================================================
HOW IT WORKS
================================================================================

Character Limit: 320 characters per message
Breaking: At word boundaries (never mid-word)

Example Response (2000 characters):
- Part 1: [Gemini] First 320 chars...
- Part 2: Next 320 chars...
- Part 3: Remaining text...

Console Shows:
[CHAT] Response formatted into 3 part(s) for {Player}
[CHAT] Response sent to {Player} in 3 part(s)
